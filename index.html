CUSTOMISED
JEWELLERY

Our staged approach of making a customised jewel helps in visualising the design and product in interim stages so that the final jewellery item aligns with your expectations.

For your customised jewel, we will select right gemstones that suit your preferences. For diamond jewellery, this interactive tool helps you in visualising a diamond's appearance with different quality characteristics.

CUSTOMISATION
STAGES

Stage-1

Select a design or create a new one with us

Stage-2

Review a 2D sketch and a 3D CAD design to visualise the end product

Stage-3

Review a wax prototype to see how your jewellery piece will look

Stage-4

Check the raw unpolished Gold/Platinum mount

Stage-5

Set gemstones and have the final jewel ready
